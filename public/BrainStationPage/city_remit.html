Overview

In response to the growing need for efficient cross-border financial transactions, City Bank recognized the importance of building a remittance app. This initiative aimed to facilitate remitters living in Malaysia, enabling them to send money seamlessly through a cross-border banking remittance channel app to various countries.

Business Objectives

  • Provide a swift and secure way to transfer money.
  • Facilitate seamless money transfers for users in Malaysia to various countries
  • Ensure cost-effective transactions with favorable exchange rates and low fees.
  • Provide timely financial support to recipients, meeting the urgent needs of users.

Key Challenges

  • Conventional remittance processes involved extensive paperwork and manual handling, leading to prolonged fund transfer timelines.
  • Delayed transactions were common with traditional methods due to rigorous verification processes and manual handling.
  • Limited access to banking facilities was a significant challenge for individuals in remote areas.
  • Users often resorted to unreliable and less secure methods for money transfers.

Solution Architecture

  • Platform: Cross-platform application (Android and iOS).
  • Process: Streamlined user registration, KYC verification, and money transfer process.
  • Security: Robust security measures, including KYC verification and transaction PINs.
  • Transaction Modes: Cash pickup, account transfer, and mobile wallet.
  • Features: Beneficiary management, currency conversion, and transaction history.
  • Support: Multiple language support for a seamless user experience.

Delivery Approach

  • Agile Methodology: The project was completed using an agile methodology to ensure flexibility and adapt to evolving client needs.
  • Iterative Releases: A multi-phase delivery with iterative releases was used to build the platform.
  • Simplified UX: A simplified registration process and intuitive design were implemented to minimize friction and ensure a seamless user experience.
  • Security by Design: Robust security measures were employed to safeguard every transfer and instill user trust.

Impact

  • The platform has achieved significant market adoption with over 50,000 downloads.
  • Users benefit from cost-effective transactions with favorable exchange rates and low fees.
  • The app ensures that financial support reaches recipients in a timely manner.
  • Stringent security measures protect transactions and sensitive information, instilling user confidence.
